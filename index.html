Serving the Community

Since 1989, Benicia's Cub Scouts, Scouts BSA, and Venture Crew BSA units recycle Christmas trees as a community service project and fundraiser. This project is run in full cooperation with the City of Benicia and Republic Services. The trees are chipped, and used for ground cover on City properties. Please take advantage of our convenient service.
